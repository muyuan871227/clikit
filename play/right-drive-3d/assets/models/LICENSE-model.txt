HERO CAR — "carconcept.glb"  (CC BY 4.0 — original, NON-branded concept supercar)
  Source: Khronos glTF Sample Assets "Car Concept" —
    https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets/tree/main/Models/CarConcept
  Artist: Eric Chadwick.  Owner: © 2024 Darmstadt Graphics Group GmbH.
  License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0), SPDX CC-BY-4.0.
    Verbatim: '© 2024, Darmstadt Graphics Group GmbH. "CC BY 4.0 International"'
    (kept as proof in carconcept.LICENSE.md + carconcept.metadata.json, copied from Khronos).
  ORIGINAL concept car — NOT a replica of any real, trademarked vehicle. (Khronos:
    "started from a public domain 3D model created by Unity Fan", optimized by E. Chadwick.)
  Modified for this game: decimated 213,347 -> 61,436 tris (meshopt simplify); textures
    <=1K WEBP; recentred bottom-at-y0; 4 wheels (WheelFrontL/R, WheelRearL/R) split into
    independently-driven meshes so the Rapier raycast vehicle can steer/spin/suspend each;
    interior Khronos emblem + licence plate hidden (no logos). Body re-skinned with PBR
    clearcoat paint. Attribution shown on the title screen + a persistent HUD credit chip.
