HERO CAR — "kenney-sedan-sports.glb"
  Source: Kenney "Car Kit" (3.1) — https://kenney.nl/assets/car-kit , author Kenney (Kenney Vleugels).
  License: CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain) — verified from the pack's License.txt
    ("License: (Creative Commons Zero, CC0)"). Generic/original low-poly car, NOT a
    real-world branded model.
  This is the pack's `sedan-sports.glb` with its sibling `Textures/colormap.png` palette
  EMBEDDED into the .glb (bufferView image) so it is a single self-contained file.
  Critically, the model ships its 4 wheels as SEPARATELY NAMED nodes
  (wheel-front-left/right, wheel-back-left/right) plus `body`+`spoiler`, which is what
  lets each wheel be driven independently by the Rapier raycast-vehicle controller
  (steer + spin + suspension), unlike a single welded body mesh.

SCENERY — all from Kenney "Racing Kit" (2.0) — https://kenney.nl/assets/racing-kit ,
  author Kenney. License: CC0 1.0 Universal (verified from the pack's License.txt).
  No textures (flat material colours), so each .glb is self-contained:
    • kenney-treeSmall.glb / kenney-treeLarge.glb — roadside low-poly trees.
    • kenney-lightPost.glb  — modern roadside light post.
    • kenney-grandStand.glb  — spectator grandstand for the start/finish straight.
